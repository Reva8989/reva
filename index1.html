Welcome to PGKM School

PGKM (Poona Gujarati Kelavani Mandal) School, located in Kondhwa, Pune, was established in 1926 with a commitment to fostering educational excellence. The school provides a vibrant learning environment that nurtures academic, cultural, and moral growth, ensuring students develop into well-rounded individuals. Affiliated with the CBSE board, it offers a dynamic curriculum designed to build conceptual understanding and critical thinking skills. The CBSE framework integrates academic rigor with co-curricular activities, preparing students for competitive exams and global challenges.
